MY NAME

My name is William Ofei-Badu. This was one given in honour of my late grandfather, my father's Dad.

MY BIO

I am Ofei-Badu William. I am an eighteen year old student in the Computer Engineering Department of KNUST. I am a native of Aburi-Mampong though currently I reside in the vicinity of Obuasi. Throughout my life, I have been a Christian. I come from a staunch Christian household as a result of my father being a Reverend Minister, Pastor Seth Asare Ofei-Badu. As such I love God and take my Christian life very seriously. My mother is a teacher who works in the government sector. By deduction, my upbringing was geared heavily towards good morals and discipline. Due to this, I consider myself as one who has undergone holistic training.

EDUCATIONAL BACKGROUND

In terms of my educational background, there isn't much to say yet. I am a proud old boy of St. Peter's Senior High School. I read General Science with Biology as an elective. So as it stands now, the real question is: "Why Computer Engineering"

WHY COMPUTER ENGINEERING

I did not want to pursue this course initially despite my inherent knack for the course. I wanted to become a medical doctor so that I would finally be respected. That childish idealogy changed when I picked a course to read in university. I realised that I was more keen on picking Computer Engineering as a second choice than I was about my first choice. God being the decider of one's chosen path, I got the course and the rest is history. I have never been happier. Though the stress associated is very much present but honestly, I wouldn't want to do anything else.
